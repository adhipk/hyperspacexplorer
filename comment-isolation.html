Comment Model

Comments are nearby HTML, not hidden metadata.

A basic comment is placed into the document structure based on the user's first click. It is not attached to a particular object. Its rendered position can float anywhere, but its source remains close to the content area where it belongs.

Why not attributes?

Full comment text in attributes makes source hard to read and awkward to edit. A real aside keeps the note readable to humans and straightforward for agents.

Durable shape

<section data-hs-comment-host>
  <h2 editmode:contenteditable>A heading</h2>

  <aside data-hs-comment movable style="transform: translate(120px, 48px)">
    <p editmode:contenteditable>This source remains readable.</p>
  </aside>
</section>
